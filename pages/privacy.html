Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Forge Faith Studios, a division of KDT Investments Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Forge Faith mobile application and website at forgefaith.net (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication).
  • Usage Data: We collect information on how you interact with the app, including devotions read, streak data, and in-app activity.
  • Prayer Requests: If you submit a prayer request to the public Prayer Wall, that content is stored and displayed publicly. Anonymous submissions display as "Anonymous."
  • Subscription Information: Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment card information. We receive confirmation of subscription status through RevenueCat.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, and push notification tokens to deliver app notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Track your devotion progress and streak data
  • Send push notifications for daily devotion reminders (with your permission)
  • Display public prayer requests on the Prayer Wall
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Respond to your questions or support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), which employs industry-standard encryption and security practices. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process data:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, and storage
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management
  • Apple App Store — App distribution and in-app purchases

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

5. Subscriptions

Forge Faith Pro is available as a monthly ($4.99/month) or annual ($34.99/year) auto-renewable subscription, processed through the Apple App Store. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple ID account settings.

By subscribing, you also agree to Apple's Terms of Service: https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/us/terms.html

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

7. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can delete your account directly within the Forge Faith app by navigating to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Deleting your account will permanently remove your personal data, streak history, and account information from our systems.

For additional data requests or questions, contact us at support@forgefaithstudios.com.

8. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications to remind you of your daily devotion. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device's notification settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
