What We Stand For

The three pillars of Forge Faith Nation

⚔️

Truth Without Apology

We don't water down Scripture to make it easier. We read it, wrestle with it, and let it change us. The truth is a gift, not a threat.

🔨

Hard Work as Worship

We believe that showing up — to work, to family, to faith, to the grind — is an act of worship when done with the right heart. We don't quit.

🙏

Brotherhood in Battle

Men were not designed to fight alone. We stand with each other. We pray for each other. We tell each other the truth even when it's hard.

The Code

The Forge Faith Code of Honor

These aren't rules. They're the convictions of a man who has decided to live for something worth dying for.

  • I

    I show up every day

    Not when it's convenient. Not when I feel like it. I show up when it's hard — because that's when it counts the most.

  • II

    I put God first

    Before the job, before the hustle, before the phone. Every morning belongs to God before it belongs to anything else.

  • III

    I lead my home

    My wife and my kids need a man who is present, not just providing. I will be both — and I will lead spiritually first.

  • IV

    I don't run from hard conversations

    I will tell the truth in love. I will receive truth from my brothers. I won't hide behind silence when someone needs to hear the word.

  • V

    I stand with my brothers

    When a man in this nation is struggling, I don't scroll past. I pray. I reach out. I show up. Iron sharpens iron — that's not a slogan, it's a commitment.

  • VI

    I play the long game

    I don't measure my life in quarterly results. I measure it in faithfulness — to God, to my family, to my word, to the mission He gave me.
