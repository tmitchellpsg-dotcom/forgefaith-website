The Code The Forge Faith Code of Honor These aren't rules. They're the convictions of a man who has decided to live for something worth dying for. I I show up every day Not when it's convenient. Not when I feel like it. I show up when it's hard — because that's when it counts the most. II I put God first Before the job, before the hustle, before the phone. Every morning belongs to God before it belongs to anything else. III I lead my home My wife and my kids need a man who is present, not just providing. I will be both — and I will lead spiritually first. IV I don't run from hard conversations I will tell the truth in love. I will receive truth from my brothers. I won't hide behind silence when someone needs to hear the word. V I stand with my brothers When a man in this nation is struggling, I don't scroll past. I pray. I reach out. I show up. Iron sharpens iron — that's not a slogan, it's a commitment. VI I play the long game I don't measure my life in quarterly results. I measure it in faithfulness — to God, to my family, to my word, to the mission He gave me. Brothers Speaking From the Nation ★★★★★ "Day 1 hit me like a freight train.
